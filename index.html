About Me

I became extremely excited with statistics and data analytics early last year when the Coronavirus pandemic first hit. While in lockdown, I learned how vital data is. It is fascinating that implementing an EHR (Electronic Health Records) has digitized the health industry, and the coronavirus pandemic has been one of the examples of its utilization. During the past summer of 2021, I have completed multiple data science course certifications. I have experience in several data analysis tools, such as R, Phyton (NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib), MySQL, Tableau, Jupyter Notebook, IBM Watson Studio, Microsoft Office (Excel/VBA, PowerPoint, Word), and HTML.
